# Changelog

## 0.1.0 — Initial cut

First public-shaped release. Forged out of `ASS-ADE-SEED` after isolating
the absorb-and-emerge wedge from the broader Atomadic surface.

### Verbs shipped
- `forge auto` — scout + cherry + assimilate + wire + certify in one shot.
- `forge recon` / `forge cherry` / `forge finalize` — granular workflow.
- `forge wire` — upward-import scanner.
- `forge certify` — documentation/tests/layout/imports score.
- `forge doctor` — environment diagnostic.
- Specialty: `forge emergent`, `forge synergy`, `forge commandsmith`,
  `forge feature-then-emergent` (lazily registered).

### Known limits (also in README)
- Python only.
- Auto output is bootstrapped material, not finished software.
- No semantic merge across conflicting symbols (rename / first / last / fail).
- Cryptographic certificate signing chain ships in 0.2.

### Dropped from the parent
- `chat`, `voice`, `agent`, `a2a` → moving to Atomadic Assistant.
- `pay`, `wallet`, `vrf`, `defi`, `mev`, `vanguard`, `aegis`, `bitnet`
  → stay in AAAA-Nexus.
- `discord`, `hello`, `heartbeat` → infrastructure, not Forge surface.
